Jordan's Thoughts on Pizza

I like pizza. It is so tasty. When I bite into it, my mouth is filled with happiness.

I first made my own pizza when I was a teenager. I would invite all of my friends over, and we would make our own dough and create individual pizzas with different toppings.

Steps to make pizza:

  1. make the dough
  2. knead the dough
  3. form the dough into a circle, tossing it in air
  4. put on pizza pan and add toppings
  5. bake it at 400 degrees for 12 minutes

Sample pizza toppings:

  • tomato sauce
  • cheese
  • sausage
  • pineapple
  • onions
